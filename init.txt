Init.fnl
------------------------------------------------------------
This starts the compiler and publisher.

[main]
    (local compiler (require :lib.compiler))
    (local publisher (require :lib.publisher))
    
    (when (= (length arg) 1)
        (compiler (. arg 1) (publisher (. arg 1))))
[/]
